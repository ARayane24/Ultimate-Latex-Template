\section{TOC Features Demo}

\subsection{Section and Subsection Numbering}
The TOC dynamically updates:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Section numbers can be Roman (\UseRomantrue) or Arabic (\UseRomanfalse)
    \item Page numbers and section numbers are colored based on your theme
    \item TOC supports LTR and RTL languages
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Color Customization}
You can override TOC colors in `settings.tex` like this:

\begin{verbatim}
\setTOCColors{
    section=red,
    subsection=green,
    subsubsection=orange,
    number=blue,
    roman=true,
    title=purple
}
\end{verbatim}

This will immediately reflect in your TOC.

\subsection{RTL Demo}
If RTL is enabled, the TOC title and numbering will automatically align to the right, and Arabic content will display correctly.
